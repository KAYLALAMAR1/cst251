Lab 12: Advanced Block Level Styling 2

Subject of This Lab

The purpose of this lab is to experiment with block level styling by centering and floating things.

Challenges

While completing this lab, I ran into some trouble with centering one of the images. I was able do correctly do one of them, but the other was in the wrong place.

Results

As I searched for what went wrong, I realized that I forgot to add the margins. I swifty resolved this issue by adding the left and right margins and their values needed to center the image.
