Lab 14: Typography 2

Subject of This Lab

The subject of this lab is to use block level and typography css to replicate a more challenging webpage.

Challenges

During this lab, I had some difficulties with trying to get the text that says "on the beach or on a hike" to be in the right position. It's supposed to be right above the "75%", but it only stayed at the top of the page. I tried multiple things, however none of them worked because whenever I tried to move it downwards, all of the text on the page moved downwards as well. So the distance between the text I was trying to move and the "75%" never closed.

Results

After searching for answers for a couple of days, I finally came across a solution. I completely forgot about "position: absoute" and was struggling because of this one thing. So I put it in my css, then changed the margins and everything started working. I was able to replicate the the spotify page after fixing this problem.
