Lab 9: Styling Block Elements

Subject of This Lab

The subject of this lab is to use a css file to style a webpage by modifying the margins, padding, border, and color.

Challenges

While completing this lab I had trouble with centering my image. I tried setting the left and right margins to auto, but it wasn't working.

Results

I fixed this issue by entering the amount of pixels for both the left and right margins. After changing the image size, I increased and decreased the values until the image was centered.
